Hi, my name is Bozhidar. This is my sneakerblog

I’ve been a sneakerhead for as long as I can remember. Sneakers have always been more than just shoes to me—they’re an art form, a culture, and a way to express who I am. From my first pair of Air Jordans to the limited-edition collabs I’ve hunted down, every pair tells a story.

But being a sneakerhead isn’t just about collecting—it’s about the chase, the history, and the community. I’ve spent countless hours researching colorways, lining up for drops, and connecting with other sneaker enthusiasts who share the same passion.

Why I started this blog

I wanted a space where I could share my love for sneakers. Whether it’s breaking down the story behind an iconic release, reviewing the latest drops, or offering tips on how to keep your grails fresh, this blog is my way of giving back to the sneaker community that has given me so much.

My goal is to inspire, educate, and connect with other sneaker lovers like you. Whether you’re a seasoned collector or just starting your sneaker journey, I hope you’ll find something here to fuel your passion.

What you can expect

This blog is all about sneakers—pure and simple. From reviews and styling tips to insider stories and tips on how to snag those impossible-to-get pairs, I’ll cover it all. Plus, I’ll share personal stories about my own collection and what makes each pair special to me.

If you love sneakers as much as I do, you’re in the right place. Welcome to the community—let’s talk kicks!
